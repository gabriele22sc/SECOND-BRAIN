## **SELF-AWARNESS: WHO AM I?**

Have you ever wondered why you react a certain way to situations? Or what drives you to make certain decisions?

Just like a computer program follows instructions, our actions come from our beliefs, emotions, and habits. But having many experiences in life does not mean we understand ourselves. **Self-awareness** happens when we take time to think about our experiences and learn from them. It is like checking a program for mistakes to make it work better. Analyzing actions is a part of a process that involves different steps:

-      **Your beliefs and principles:** Like rules in a program, our beliefs guide our decisions and actions. Knowing them helps us understand why we do things.

-      **What you value and what is important to you:** In programming, we organize tasks by priority. In life, knowing what is important helps us focus on the right things.

-      **What motivates you:** Every function in a program has a purpose. Understanding what pushes us forward helps us make better choices.

-      **Your own emotions:** Emotions are like system responses. If we understand them, we can control our reactions better.

-      **Your thinking patterns:** Just like we improve a program by finding mistakes, we can improve our decisions by knowing how we think.

-      **Your tendencies to react to certain situations:** If we understand how we usually react, we can change bad habits and improve our responses.

-      **What you want out of life:** A program needs a goal to work well. In the same way, having clear goals helps us move in the right direction.

For conclusion, I think that sometimes we got stop and reflect about our actions in the past to create a better future for.
